Trash Collector

RBE 2002 Project
Oct 2024 - Dec 2024

For my RBE 2002 class, my team and I were tasked with building a robot capable of navigating a course, locating and picking up trash, weighing it, carrying it up a ramp, and depositing it.

Sensors

The robot completes its tasks using a line sensor, gyroscope, accelerometer, computer vision, a load cell, and a lifter arm, all connected to an Arduino board.

Camera & Computer Vision

Chippy is made out of 3D printed TPU, which is super flexible and good at absorbing hits.

Additionally, it features machined AR500 forks and blades, as well as carbon fiber and UHWM parts.

Soldering & Electronics

Chippy is powered by four brushless motors that control the drive, arm, and weapon blade. Each motor is soldered to its own electronic speed controller (ESC), which interfaces with a remote control for operation.
