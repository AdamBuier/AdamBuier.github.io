Walker Robot

Nov 2024 - Jan 2025

Walker is a 2-DOF walking robot designed to demonstrate efficient kinematics and coordinated motion. It was developed as part of a robotics project to explore autonomous walking mechanisms and obstacle traversal.

CAD & Design

The Walker robot was designed using Onshape and SolidWorks. Stress simulations were conducted to ensure the robot's structural integrity during walking and obstacle traversal.

Materials

Walker is constructed using 3D-printed TPU for flexibility and impact absorption. Additional components include carbon fiber, UHMW plastic, and machined aluminum parts for durability and lightweight performance.

Soldering & Electronics

Walker is powered by brushless motors controlled by electronic speed controllers (ESCs). The electronics are soldered and configured to enable precise motor control for walking and obstacle navigation.
