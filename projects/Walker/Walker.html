Walker Robot


Nov 2024 - Jan 2025

Chippy is a 3lb hammersaw style combat robot designed to strike and break the top of an opponents bot. It has competed at the National Havoc Robot League (NHRL) twice and currently holds a record of 4-2.

CAD & Design

I used Onshape and Solidworks to CAD Chippy as well as run stress simulations to ensure the robots functionality.

Chippy has a custom weapon motor designed to withstand the high forces created when striking a bot

Materials

Chippy is made out of 3D printed TPU, which is super flexible and good at absorbing hits.

Additionally, it features machined AR500 forks and blades, as well as carbon fiber and UHWM parts.

Soldering & Electronics

Chippy is powered by four brushless motors that control the drive, arm, and weapon blade. Each motor is soldered to its own electronic speed controller (ESC), which interfaces with a remote control for operation.
