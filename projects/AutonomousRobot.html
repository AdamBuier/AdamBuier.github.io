CHIPPY

Beetleweight Combat Robot
April 2024 - October 2024

Chippy is a 3 lb hammersaw robot designed for high-impact precision strikes on opponents from above.

The robot incorporates a custom-designed arm mechanism and modified motor to maximize its destructive potential, ensuring Chippy remains competitive in any scenario.

The Challenge

We had to build a robot to gather and deliver colored balls across an arena divided by a plexiglass barrier with openings. Red and blue balls were sent to the opponent’s side, while we collected yellow balls.

Robots operated both autonomously and via remote control to complete these tasks.

Design

Our final design used an elevator and ramp mechanism, allowing efficient ball transport with speed and reliability.

A conveyor belt with rigid flaps lifted and launched the balls, minimizing failure points and boosting performance.

Arm Mechanism

Chippy’s arm mechanism was CADed and simulated to ensure it could withstand the stresses of high-speed combat.

The design process involved optimizing the arm's strength and movement to ensure it could deliver powerful strikes without breaking.

3D Printing

Most of Chippy's components are 3D printed from flexible TPU, chosen for its durability and flexibility.

A 3D printing slicer was used to optimize the parts' strength while keeping them lightweight, ensuring Chippy remains agile and resilient in combat.
