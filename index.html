Chippy

Home > Projects > Chippy
Category: Combat Robot
Status: Active
Started: 2023
Team Size: 4 people

Silent Spring is a heavyweight combat robot designed for the BattleBots competition. It features a unique spring-loaded flipper mechanism that can toss opponents into the air.

The robot was designed with two main goals in mind: create a formidable weapon system that could disable opponents quickly, and develop a highly maneuverable platform that could outpace other heavyweights in the arena.

Features

  • Pneumatic flipper capable of lifting 250 lbs
  • Custom armor plating designed to withstand impacts
  • Advanced drive system for agile movement
  • Remote control system with fail-safes
  • Lightweight chassis that maximizes power-to-weight ratio

Development Journey

Silent Spring went through three major design iterations. The first prototype suffered from power delivery issues, which we solved in the second version by implementing a more efficient pneumatic system. The current version has refined armor placement and improved electronics protection.

One of the biggest challenges we faced was balancing the weight distribution. The pneumatic system is heavy, but we needed to maintain maneuverability. After several prototypes, we found the optimal placement that allows for both a powerful flipper and responsive driving.
