About Me

Hi there! My name is Adam Buier, and welcome to my portfolio.

I’m pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Robotics and Mechanical Engineering, passionate about designing and building robots. I enjoy doing art, bouldering, and working on new robotics projects in my free time.

This past summer, I interned at the Wisconsin HTS Axisymmetric Mirror as an engineering intern, where I contributed to building a nuclear fusion reactor.

My goal is to apply my engineering skills to cutting-edge technologies and help shape the future of robotics.

Outside The Box Podcast

I was featured on The Outside Of The Box podcast, where I discussed building and competing with my 3lb combat robot, Chippy. I shared insights into its performance at an April tournament, including what worked and what needed improvement.
